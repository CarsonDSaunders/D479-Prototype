Activities

(A green plant with yellow flowers in front of a mountain, 2022)

Taniti is a paradise for both adventurers and those looking to relax. Explore the island’s natural beauty, dive into its rich culture, or enjoy modern entertainment options.

Beaches & Water Activities

Spend your days soaking up the sun on Taniti's pristine beaches. Visitors can swim, snorkel, or charter a boat for a fishing tour around Yellow Leaf Bay. For the adventurous, scuba diving around the island's coral reefs offers a stunning view of underwater life.

Rainforest Exploration

Take a guided hike through Taniti’s lush rainforest, home to diverse plant and animal species. For a more thrilling adventure, try zip-lining through the jungle canopy or taking a helicopter ride for breathtaking views of the island.

Cultural Experiences

Visit Taniti's local history museum and learn about the island’s indigenous culture, or explore its art galleries showcasing local artists. You can also experience the island's vibrant nightlife, including local pubs, a microbrewery, and a newly opened dance club.

Other Activities

Entertainment doesn’t stop at the beach! Check out the island’s arcade, bowling alley, or the upcoming nine-hole golf course set to open next year. Whether you prefer a laid-back evening or an active day, Taniti offers something for every traveler.
