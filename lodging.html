Lodging

(A body of water that has some houses on it, 2023)

Whether you’re looking for a luxurious getaway or a budget-friendly stay, Taniti offers a variety of accommodations to suit your needs.

Four-Star Resort

Taniti Resort & Spa – For those seeking luxury, this four-star resort offers beachfront views, high-end dining, and a full-service spa. Ideal for couples or families looking for an upscale experience.

Family-Owned Hotels

Taniti is home to many small, family-owned hotels that offer a personal touch and warm, friendly service. Perfect for those looking for a more intimate experience, these hotels are spread across the island, giving you access to both beach and mountain views.

Bed & Breakfasts

With the growing number of bed-and-breakfasts on the island, travelers can enjoy cozy accommodations with the bonus of a homemade breakfast each morning. These B&Bs are a great option for visitors looking for charm and comfort at an affordable price.

Hostel

For budget-conscious travelers, Taniti has an inexpensive hostel that offers clean rooms, a communal kitchen, and a friendly atmosphere for backpackers and adventurers alike.

All lodging on Taniti is regulated and regularly inspected to ensure a high standard of cleanliness and safety for visitors..
