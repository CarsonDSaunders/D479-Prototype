Dining

Taniti's dining scene offers a rich blend of local and international flavors, ensuring that every palate is satisfied.

Local Cuisine

Experience the best of Taniti’s fresh seafood with five restaurants specializing in local fish dishes paired with rice and native spices. Whether you’re enjoying grilled mahi-mahi by the beach or a hearty fish stew, the island's local flavors will leave you craving more.

American-Style Dining

If you're craving familiar flavors, three restaurants on the island offer American-style meals. From burgers and fries to classic comfort food, these spots are perfect for families or anyone looking for a taste of home.

Pan-Asian Cuisine

For those seeking international flair, Taniti boasts two Pan-Asian restaurants that serve a fusion of flavors from across the continent. Enjoy sushi, stir-fried noodles, or savory rice dishes with a tropical twist.

Beverages

On Taniti, you can enjoy a variety of beverages, from fresh fruit juices to locally brewed beers. Don’t miss the island’s signature cocktail, the Taniti Sunset, a refreshing blend of tropical fruits and rum. Find more information about Taniti's laws on alcohol consumption on our Visitor Information page.
